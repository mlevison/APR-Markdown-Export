Agile Teams need to make small decisions frequently. Many teams experiment with majority rules and quickly discover that it's a way to ensure a few team members feel ignored. Usually it's the quiet people who get missed.

For Yes/No decisions both [Fist of five](external:https://helpingimprove.com/learning-with-fist-of-five-voting/) and [Decider protocol](external:https://thecoreprotocols.org/protocols/decider.html) can work well. Dot voting is a tool for prioritizing among a group of items.
